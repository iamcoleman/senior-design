Sentiment Analysis Tool

Background

There are plenty of tools that allow users to assess the amount of interest in a topic over time but provide no insight as to how people feel about that topic. To Google Trends, there is no difference between a spike of excitement and a spike of anger. As a consumer, brand manager, or investor, gauging sentiment can be as important as gauging interest, so this is a crucial lack of information.
